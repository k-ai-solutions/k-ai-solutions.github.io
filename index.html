Frequently Asked Questions

How can Kubernetes help reduce cloud costs?

Kubernetes allows for efficient bin-packing of containers, meaning you can run more applications on fewer servers. Combined with horizontal autoscaling (HPA) and spot instances, we typically help clients reduce their AWS/Azure bills by 30-50%.

What is GitOps and why do I need it?

GitOps is a modern operational framework that takes DevOps best practices used for application development, such as version control and CI/CD, and applies them to infrastructure automation. It ensures your infrastructure is reproducible, auditable, and easy to roll back.

Do you provide training for internal teams?

Yes. We believe in "enablement, not dependency." We provide hands-on training for your team on Kubernetes, Terraform, and DevOps practices so they can confidently manage the platform after our engagement ends.
